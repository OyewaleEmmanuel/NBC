ABOUT NBC
The Nigeria Baptist Convention, which Thomas Jefferson Bowen started in 1850,has grown into over 10,000 churches with
about 3,000,000 baptized members and up to 6.5 million worshipping members spread across the nation. This denominational
entity has metamorphosed into thirty-one conferences(ecclesiastical regions) in Nigeria alone. The qork has spread to several
other African nations including Republic of Benin, Cote D'Ivoire,Mali,Sierra leone,Mozambique,Chad,Niger,Burkina Faso and
South Sudan. The United Kingdom and and the United States of America are also experiencing the the influence of its growth and
expansion.
